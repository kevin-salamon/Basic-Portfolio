About Me

Hello there, and welcome to my portfolio - a collection of my ongoing web-development projects and musings. I'm a former politics junkie and grad, turned pool boy, turned law office grunt, before finally discovering a passion for coding and development. It's my hope that I can turn this passion into a lifestyle, and a fulfilling career path. Please feel free to peruse my collection of projects and posts and see for yourself what I've done, what I'm doing, and any future projects. And again, welcome.
